The General Council Assembly of FYN was held on July 9th to 10th

Share

FacebookLinkedInEmail

The General Council Assembly of the вЂњUncovering the Potential of Border-Dwelling YouthвЂќ Frontline Youth Network was held on July 9th to 10th.

The assembly brought together Frontline's office, main committee, working groups, current Executive Committee members and candidates, and graduates of the Seed the Change summer school. Participants discussed work processes, strategic plans, organizational rules, and significant decisions for the network. Decisions were reached through joint consensus and voting.

The agenda included

  • Reporting on and evaluating completed work and programmes for 2021-2022.
  • Developing strategic plans for communication, cooperation, and organizational growth for 2022-2024.
  • Electing Executive Committee members for the 2022-2023 term.
  • Discussing and prioritizing the annual action plan.

All decisions were reached through shared discussion, consensus, and subsequent voting.

Participants joined in both online and offline formats. Each General Council candidate briefly presented their collaboration with Frontline and the areas where they hoped to contribute. Serine Avetisyan, Natela Navasardyan, Joe Davtyan, and Yervand Abrahamyan were re-elected to their roles in the Executive Committee during the first session.

Questions raised by staff led to productive discussions about working practices and team well-being. The assembly confirmed Secretariat employees and deliverers and discussed reorganizing teams and responsibilities to strengthen both the quality of work and a supportive working environment.

Learning democratic practice

Summer school participants were invited to observe the General Council session. This gave young people direct exposure to democratic management practices and Frontline's organizational structure, connecting the network's educational values with its own decision-making.

The General Council meets annually to elect Executive Committee members and develop strategic plans. Working meetings before and after the assembly help the team evaluate, revise, and improve Frontline's work throughout the year.
