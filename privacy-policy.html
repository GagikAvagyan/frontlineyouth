Privacy Policy

How Frontline Youth Network handles information shared through this website.

Last updated

July 17, 2026Contact us →

Frontline Youth Network respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we may collect when you use our website and how we handle it.

Information we collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, and message when you submit a contact or subscription form. We may also receive basic technical information, such as browser type and pages visited, to understand how the website is used.

How we use information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, provide requested updates, manage subscriptions, improve our website, and communicate about Frontline Youth Network activities. We do not sell personal information.

Sharing information

We may use trusted service providers to support website hosting, email delivery, and analytics. They may process information only as needed to provide those services. We may also disclose information when required by law.

Data retention and security

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected. We take appropriate steps to protect information, although no online service can guarantee complete security.

Your choices

You may ask us to update or delete personal information you have provided. You may also unsubscribe from email communications at any time using the link in an email or by contacting us.

External links

Our website may link to third-party websites and services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, and Frontline Youth Network is not responsible for their content or data practices.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when our website or practices change. The latest version will always appear on this page with its updated date.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please use our Contact Us page.
